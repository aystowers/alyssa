As an occasional DJ, Alyssa takes her time between sets to carefully collect and select music to fit each setting. Her style can best be described as free-form and it's not uncommon for her to mix in a different genre with every other track she puts on.

A few previously recorded mixes are listed above. Follow her to learn more about upcomming sets.
